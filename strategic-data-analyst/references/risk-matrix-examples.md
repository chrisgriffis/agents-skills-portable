# Risk Matrix: Worked Placements

How to place an observation on the suspicion x relevance matrix when the call is
non-obvious. Risk = relevance x suspicion; validation effort goes where the
product is largest (the VALIDATE-NOW quadrant).

```
              high relevance
   VALIDATE NOW  |  ACTIONABLE
  high susp -----+----- low susp
   QUARANTINE    |  AMBIENT
              low relevance
```

## Placement examples

**A single secondhand claim that an ally is leaving.** High relevance (it would
move several arcs), high suspicion (one hop, unconfirmed). -> VALIDATE NOW. Drive
suspicion down fast: seek a second independent source, a mechanism, a direct
check. Do not act and do not discard.

**A confirmed deadline shift, signed and circulated.** High relevance, low
suspicion. -> ACTIONABLE. Promote and serve downstream with its confidence.

**An offhand stylistic tic in one actor's email.** Low relevance, high suspicion.
-> QUARANTINE. Retain it (never discard), but spend no cycles until its relevance
changes.

**A well-sourced fact that touches nothing in play.** Low relevance, low
suspicion. -> AMBIENT. Note it; no spend.

## The precise-source ambiguity (meta example)

A source whose register is surgical emits something that could be a deliberate
contrast or an error. Example: a priorities list reads "methodical incompetent
preparation as a hedge and predictor of success." Two readings:

- SIGNAL: a deliberate paradox -- deliberately incompetent prep as some perverse
  hedge. Coherent enough to be intentional given the source's precision.
- NOISE: a dictation slip for "methodical and competent preparation."

Placement: high relevance (it changes a core scoring criterion), high suspicion
(genuinely ambiguous). -> VALIDATE NOW.

Resolution discipline:

1. Do not auto-correct (would destroy the signal if it was deliberate).
2. Do not auto-accept (would propagate noise if it was a slip).
3. Carry both readings, dated, and seek the disambiguating evidence -- here, ask
   the source. Reversibility makes holding both free until one collapses.

In this case the source confirmed the slip: "and competent." The correct move
was to hold, not to guess. The cost of guessing wrong on a high-relevance core
criterion is exactly the risk the matrix exists to minimize.
